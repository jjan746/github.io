Privacy Policy for Sudoku Adventure

Effective Date: February 10, 2026
Last Updated: February 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the mobile game Sudoku Adventure (hereinafter referred to as "the Game", "we", "our", or "us") available on Google Play. We respect your privacy. Sudoku Adventure is designed as a fully offline single-player game and does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal or non-personal information from users.

Specifically, Sudoku Adventure:

  • Does not require account registration
  • Does not collect your name, email, phone number, or address
  • Does not access contacts, photos, files, or device storage
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use cookies
  • Does not use device identifiers
  • Does not log gameplay data on any server

All gameplay happens locally on your device.

2. Advertising

Sudoku Adventure does not display ads and does not integrate any advertising networks.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, tracking services, or external servers. No data is shared with any third party.

4. Children’s Privacy

Since Sudoku Adventure does not collect any information, it does not knowingly collect data from children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country). The Game is safe for general audiences.

5. Data Storage

All game progress and settings (if any) are stored only on your device. We do not have access to this information. If you uninstall the Game, any locally stored data may be deleted by your device.

6. Data Security

Because no user data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of personal data exposure from our side.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the Game’s features change in the future. Any updates will be reflected by revising the "Last Updated" date.
